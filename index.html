About

Selected Work three projects

Featured · Solo project · 2025

Alphinland

A first-person narrative walking sim about grief, memory, and the worlds we build to survive them. Built solo in Unity over one month, inspired by Margaret Atwood's short story. Features a three-tier interaction system inspired by Resident Evil, a dual-world transition with a custom shader, and a diegetic grief meter that gates story progression without HUD explanation.

Team · Mixed Reality · 2024

Cooperative tower defense for a 6×6 m projected mixed-reality playspace with HTC Vive trackers. Resource gathering is mechanically impossible alone — both players must act simultaneously.
